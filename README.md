## Pong (Completed) ✅

- A simple arcade game, you have a ping pong ball and you play against the Enemy AI. If the ping pong ball touches you or the enemy, it adds up to their score. If it touches either wall the game ends, the score resets, and you have the ability to play again.

## Breakout (Completed) ✅

- Once again, a simple arcade game, where you have a ball which bounces of the player (basically the paddle), and hits one of the blocks. Once a block is hit the score text updates to show the score, it is worth nothing that each block hit adds 10 to your score.
- There is also the lives text, the way this works is if the ball touches the barrier below the player, a life is subtracted from your total 3 lives. Once you reach 0 lives, a "Game Over!" screen appears, where you have the possibility to play again.
- If your score reaches the max amount possible (240, as there are 24 blocks in total), a "You have done it!" screen appears, again with the possibility to click play again.
